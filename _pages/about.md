About me
======
I am a machine learning researcher who focuses on reinforcement learning for robotics.
My research goal is to make smart robots that are useful to everybody.
I am from Thailand and have been living in Japan since 2012. 

Technical interests
======
- Machine learning, especially reinforcement learning
- Robotics, especially manipulators and humanoids
- Currently interested in RL algorithms that quickly adapt large behavioral models to unseen robotics tasks.
